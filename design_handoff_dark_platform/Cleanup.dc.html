↻ WHAT CHANGED — 7 fixes
01
Drop the grid background
The 44px grid texture fights everything on top of it. A flat surface lets content breathe.
02
Kill the glows & gradients
Remove text-shadow, the focus glow ring, gradient cards and the shadow-card stack. Use one hairline border instead.
03
Flatten the surface layers
Three stacked tones (surface / 2 / 3) plus cards-in-a-grid create boxes inside boxes. Separate sections with whitespace and thin rules.
04
Reserve monospace for data
Mono + UPPERCASE + letter-spacing on every label reads as noise. Keep mono for IOCs, hashes and IDs only; labels go sentence-case sans.
05
One calm verdict line
Replace the glowing severity pill with a small dot + word + confidence. Lead with the summary at a readable size.
06
Mute the accent & severity colors
High-chroma cyan and 5 saturated severity hues compete for attention. Lower the chroma; let red mean critical and nothing else shout.
07
Calm the motion
Drop the blinking cursor and pulsing dot from the persistent report view. Reserve animation for the moment the agent is actively running.
